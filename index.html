ABOUT THE SHOP

Regulus Arms specializes in firearm customization and manufacturing, offering precision laser engraving, high-quality UV printing on PMAGs, pistol grips, and other firearm accessories, as well as manufacturing AR-15 lower receivers. Our UV printing isn't a sticker or wrap — it's printed directly onto the surface with durable UV-cured inks for a vibrant, long-lasting finish, while our laser engraving creates crisp, permanent markings on metal.
